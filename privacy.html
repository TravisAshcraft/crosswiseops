Privacy Policy

CrossWise Ops

This Privacy Policy explains how CrossWise Ops (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the CrossWise Ops app and related services (the “Service”).

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and information generated by your use of the Service, such as:

  • Account information (e.g., name, email address, role)
  • Operational data you enter (e.g., time entries, task updates, inventory logs, assets, maintenance tickets)
  • Usage data (e.g., basic diagnostics, feature usage, and performance data to keep the Service reliable)

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service (time tracking, approvals, inventory, projects, maintenance)
  • Authenticate users and enforce role-based access
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important updates

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to:

  • Operate the Service with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality obligations
  • Comply with legal requirements or protect the rights and safety of users and the Service

Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (such as auditability of time entries and approvals), unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Choices and Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from inside the app under Settings. Deleting your account removes your personal account data from the Service, subject to limited retention where required for legal compliance or operational audit records.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data using administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13.
